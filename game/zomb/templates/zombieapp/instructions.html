Instructions:

You begin the game on a street with many houses

Each house contains a certain amount of rooms

Each room can contain some of the following: ammo, food, other survivors and zombies

Other survivors and ammo helps you fight zombies, food helps you survive

When you encounter a zombie, you may fight or run away

At the end of each day, you eat some food (the more survivors you have, the more food you need)

Your goal is to kill as many zombies and survive as many days as you can
